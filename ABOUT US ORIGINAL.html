ABOUT US

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Addition Mile was founded by Chef S. Poo who sought to create an exceptional dining experience. The restaurant's name reflects core philosophy: gaining an extra mile in every aspect.

OUR VISION

To nourish our guests with exceptional food and service, fostering a sense of community and connection. We strive to create lasting memories through our inviting atmosphere to excellence.

OUR MISSION

To be more than a restaurant; to be a vibrant hub where people gather, connect, and celebrate. We aim to deliver outstanding food and genuine care, exceeding expectations.
